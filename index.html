Comparing Language Models

Nowadays language models are the most powerful instrument to transfer knowledge. Mostly pre-trained neural network models are more accurate in any type of tasks. This tendency in language processing - usage of LM weights as a part of base model weights - took place after word2vec announcing. Today there are three main ways to use pre-trained LM in different NLP tasks.

  • Use pre-trained LM as universal embedder for text/sentence
  • Fit pre-trained LM on new data (domain adaptation) and also use as an embedder
  • Fit pre-trained LM as a part of more complex and specific model

It is important to note that the resulting model is very dependent on the quality of the based LM. So a possibility to compare models is also demanded and we propose a new comprehensive way to explore properties of different LMs.
